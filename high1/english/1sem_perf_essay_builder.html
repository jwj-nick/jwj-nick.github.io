Step 2 Outline (개요 짜기)

Essay structure에 맞춰 뼈대를 영어로 적어보세요. 한 줄씩만.

📌 예시 — W1 (AI & Jobs) Intro: "AI will create more opportunities than it takes away."
Body1: Reason 1 — Human-only skills (creativity, empathy) survive. Example — teachers, nurses.
Body2: Reason 2 — AI generates new jobs. Source — WEF Report 2023: 69M new roles by 2027.
Conclusion: AI is not the end of work but its transformation.
